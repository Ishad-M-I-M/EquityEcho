# Privacy Policy

**EquityEcho**
*Last updated: April 4, 2026*

Mohamed Ishad ("we", "us", or "our") built the EquityEcho app (the "App") as an open-source application licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 SMS Messages

EquityEcho requests the `READ_SMS` and `RECEIVE_SMS` Android permissions to read and listen for SMS messages on your device. These messages are used **solely** to parse broker trade confirmations and fund transfer notifications from the Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE) brokers you configure within the App.

- **Only messages matching your configured broker sender addresses and templates are processed.**
- SMS content is parsed locally on your device to extract trade details (symbol, quantity, price, date) and fund transfer amounts.
- **No SMS data is transmitted, uploaded, or shared with any server, third party, or external service.**

### 1.2 Locally Stored Data

All data generated by the App — including trades, holdings, fund transfers, dividends, channel configurations, and preferences — is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device.

### 1.3 No Analytics or Tracking

EquityEcho does **not** include any analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, advertising frameworks, or tracking mechanisms. We do not collect any usage data, device identifiers, or behavioural information.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

Information extracted from SMS messages and entered manually is used exclusively to:

- Record buy and sell trades for your equity portfolio.
- Record fund deposits and withdrawals.
- Calculate holdings, realized gains/losses, and dividends.
- Display your portfolio dashboard and activity history.

All processing happens **entirely on your device**. No data leaves your phone.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database managed by the Drift library.
- We do not operate any servers, cloud databases, or remote APIs.
- Your data is as secure as your device itself. We recommend using device-level security features such as screen lock, encryption, and biometric authentication.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, rent, or disclose any of your data to any third party. Since no data is transmitted off your device, there is no data to share.

---

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

- Your data remains on your device for as long as you use the App.
- You can delete individual entries (trades, fund transfers, dividends) from within the App. Deleted entries are soft-deleted and can be restored from the Deleted Entries screen, or permanently removed.
- You can use the **"Clear All Data"** option in Settings to permanently delete all trades and fund transfers.
- Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data from your device.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

EquityEcho is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has used this App and you have concerns, please contact us.

---

## 7. Permissions Summary

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `READ_SMS` | Read existing SMS messages to parse broker trade and fund transfer confirmations |
| `RECEIVE_SMS` | Listen for incoming SMS messages in real time to automatically record new trades |

Both permissions are optional. You may use the App in manual-entry mode without granting SMS permissions. SMS permissions can be revoked at any time through your device's Settings.
